  • Enfoque constante en Ingenieria (Blame-free)

  • Manejo de Cambios
  • Prevision de demanda y capacidad
  • Aprovisionamiento
  • Efficiencia y Performance
Tenets of SRE: Ensuring a Durable Focus on Engineering Blame-free postmortem culture Ops 50% Eng 50% Pursuing Maximum Change Velocity Without Violating a Service’s SLO 100% wrong reliability target Error Budget, spend to get velocity Monitoring Health y availability A system that requires a human to read an email and decide whether or not some type of action needs to be taken in response is fundamentally flawed Instead, software should do the interpreting, and humans should be notified only when they need to take action.
